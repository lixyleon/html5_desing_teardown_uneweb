Person Of The Week

Stephanie Troeth (@sniffles) is a strategist, researcher, writer, and speaker who helps people create evidence-based design that is user-centered and benefits the business bottom line. Most recently, she was Head of Research at Clearleft. Before that, she led design research at the Telegraph and spearheaded European customer research with MailChimp as a research consultant. Steph also took leading roles in grassroots initiatives for a fairer and better web through work on best practices, standards, and accessibility. Thank you for everything you do for the community, dear Steph!
